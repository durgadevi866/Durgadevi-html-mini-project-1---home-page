Our Mission

The Food Allergen Detector was created to help individuals with food allergies make safer and more informed decisions. Millions of people worldwide suffer from allergies to common foods such as peanuts, milk, eggs, and wheat. Even a small amount can trigger severe reactions.

What This Tool Detects

We currently scan for the following allergens:

  • Peanuts
  • Tree Nuts (almonds, cashews, walnuts, etc.)
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Wheat
  • Soy
  • Fish
  • Shellfish

Who Can Use This?

This tool is designed for:

  • Individuals with food allergies
  • Parents checking food for children
  • Restaurants and food service providers
  • Students working on food safety projects
